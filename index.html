My services

What Me do?

When you work with me, you’re hiring a skilled cybersecurity professional with expertise in:

Bug Bounty Hunting -> I identify and report vulnerabilities in your systems, applications, and websites before malicious actors exploit them. This proactive approach ensures your platforms remain secure and trustworthy.

Penetration Testing -> I simulate real-world cyberattacks to uncover weaknesses in your systems and provide detailed recommendations to strengthen your defenses.

Server Installation -> I provide comprehensive server installation services, ensuring your infrastructure is set up securely and efficiently. This includes configuring firewalls, optimizing server performance, implementing data protection protocols, and applying best practices to ensure a robust and secure foundation for your business operations.

Cybersecurity Consulting -> I provide tailored advice and solutions to improve your security posture, whether it’s designing secure networks, implementing authentication systems, or preparing for compliance audits.

CTF Creation -> I design and develop custom Capture The Flag (CTF) challenges that test and enhance participants' cybersecurity skills. Whether you're preparing for a competition or training your team, I provide unique, engaging, and educational challenges to strengthen problem-solving and technical abilities.

By working with me, you’ll receive customized solutions to protect your business. Once the project is complete, your systems will be fortified and ready to withstand evolving cyber threats.
